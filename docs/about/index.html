    Hi

    Welcome! I’m Serge, the artist behind ‘Art By Serge’. Nestled between tradition and innovation, my work reflects a blend of the familiar and the unexpected.

    I’ve always believed that art needs to be experienced in person to truly appreciate its depth and texture, which is why I don’t advertise online. Instead, I showcase and sell my work at local venues, giving you the chance to see each piece up close. If you’re interested in seeing my artwork, the best way is to meet me at the Alpharetta Art in the Park. Swing by, and I invite you to explore and connect with my creations.

    Thanks for visiting.

    Oh, and — please, feel free to reach out to me directly — if you’re here, you likely know how to get in touch.

    I am deeply grateful to everyone who follows me and loves my art. Your support and encouragement mean everything to me.

    Serge
